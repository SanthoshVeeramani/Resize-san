Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Resize-san

Is this image resizer completely free to use?

Yes! Resize-san is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, no premium tiers, and no registration required. You can resize, convert, and compress as many images as you need, anytime.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No! All image processing happens 100% in your browser using advanced JavaScript APIs. Your images never leave your device. This makes Resize-san completely private and secure.

How do I resize multiple images at once?

Simply drag and drop multiple images into the upload area, or click to select multiple files. All selected images will be processed with the same settings. You can download them individually or as a ZIP file.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, and WEBP formats. You can convert between any of these formats: JPG to PNG, PNG to JPG, JPG to WEBP, PNG to WEBP, WEBP to JPG, and WEBP to PNG.

How do I maintain image quality when resizing?

Use the quality slider to set a higher percentage (80-100%). Higher quality means larger file sizes. For web use, 80% is usually a good balance between quality and file size.

What is the maximum file size I can upload?

You can upload images up to 50MB each. The processing happens in your browser, so the actual limit depends on your device's available memory.

Does resizing images cause quality loss?

When resizing to smaller dimensions, some quality loss is inevitable. However, our advanced algorithms minimize this loss. Using high quality settings and the lock aspect ratio feature helps maintain the best possible quality.

Can I use Resize-san on mobile devices?

Yes! Resize-san is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones and tablets. The entire experience is mobile-friendly with touch-friendly controls.

What are the best image sizes for social media?

Different platforms have different optimal sizes. Instagram posts are typically 1080x1080, Facebook covers are 820x312, YouTube thumbnails are 1280x720, and Twitter headers are 1500x500. Use our one-click presets for perfect sizing!

Is my data secure when using Resize-san?

Absolutely! Since all processing happens in your browser, your images are never uploaded anywhere. There's no server storage, no cloud processing, and no data transmission. Your privacy is 100% protected.

How do I convert PNG to JPG format?

Select the "Format Converter" tab, upload your PNG image(s), choose "JPG" from the format dropdown, adjust quality if needed, and click Download. It's that simple!

Can I compress images without losing quality?

Our compressor uses smart algorithms to reduce file size while maintaining visual quality. Use the "Balanced" setting for the best ratio. For print, use higher quality; for web, balanced works great.

Do you offer batch processing?

Yes! You can upload multiple images at once and process them all with the same settings. Download them individually or use the "Download ZIP" button to get all processed images at once.

What browsers are supported?

Resize-san works best in modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. We use standard browser APIs for maximum compatibility.

How do the social media presets work?

Simply click any preset button (Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, etc.) and then click "Use with Resizer". This will automatically set the correct dimensions in the Image Resizer tool for you.
