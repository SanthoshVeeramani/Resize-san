Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our image tools

Is this image resizer completely free to use?

Yes! Resize-san is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, no premium tiers, and no registration required.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No! All image processing happens 100% in your browser. Your images never leave your device.

How do I resize multiple images at once?

Drag and drop multiple images or hold Ctrl/Cmd to select multiple files. All will be processed with same settings.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, and WEBP. Convert between any: JPG ↔ PNG ↔ WEBP.

What is the maximum file size?

You can upload images up to 50MB each. Processing happens in your browser.

Does this work on mobile devices?

Yes! Resize-san is fully responsive and works on all devices.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! No server storage, no cloud processing. Your privacy is 100% protected.

How do the social media presets work?

Click any preset button, then click "Use with Resizer" to auto-set dimensions.

Can I batch process images?

Yes! Upload multiple images and download them individually or as a ZIP file.

What browsers are supported?

Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. We use standard browser APIs.
