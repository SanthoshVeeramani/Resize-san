Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Resize-san ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and image processing services.

2. Images You Upload

Important: Your images never leave your device.

  • All image processing happens locally in your web browser using the Canvas API
  • We do not upload, collect, store, or transmit your images to any server
  • Your images are processed entirely within your browser's memory
  • Once you close or refresh the page, your images are automatically deleted

3. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. However, we may collect:

  • Anonymous analytics: Basic usage data to understand how our tool is used (e.g., which features are popular)
  • Cookie preferences: If you choose to save your preferences locally

4. Cookies

We use minimal or no cookies. If we use any cookies, they are only for:

  • Remembering your theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Essential session functionality

5. Third-Party Services

We do not share any data with third parties. The only external services we use are:

  • Google Fonts: For typography (font files are loaded directly from Google)
  • Netlify: For website hosting (no user data is shared)

6. Data Security

Since we don't collect or store any data, there is no data breach risk. All processing happens client-side.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact page.
