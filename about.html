About Resize-san

Free, private image tools that work entirely in your browser.

Our Mission

Resize-san was created with a simple mission: to make image processing accessible to everyone without compromising privacy. We believe that image editing tools should be free, easy to use, and most importantly — respect your data.

Why Resize-san?

Most online image tools require you to upload your images to their servers. This creates privacy concerns and adds upload/download time. We built Resize-san differently — all processing happens locally in your browser using the Canvas API.

🔒 100% Private

Your images never leave your device. Everything is processed locally.

⚡ Lightning Fast

No upload or download wait times. Process images instantly.

💰 Completely Free

No hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium tiers. Always free.

📱 Works Everywhere

Works on any device with a modern browser. No app installation needed.

What We Offer

Resize-san provides a suite of free image tools:

  • Image Resizer - Resize by width, height, or percentage with aspect ratio lock
  • Format Converter - Convert between JPG, PNG, and WEBP formats
  • Image Compressor - Compress images with quality control
  • Social Media Presets - One-click presets for all major platforms

Technology

We're proud to be a static website with no backend infrastructure. Resize-san uses:

  • HTML5 and CSS3
  • Vanilla JavaScript
  • Canvas API for image processing
  • JSZip for batch downloads
